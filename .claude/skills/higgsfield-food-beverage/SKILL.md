# Seedance 2.0 on Higgsfield — Food & Beverage Video Generator

## Overview
This skill creates appetite-stimulating food and beverage video prompts for Seedance 2.0 on Higgsfield. Covers restaurant marketing, recipe content, product showcases, ASMR food, and beverage reveals.

## Prompt Structure
[Food/drink type] [Key visual appeal] [Camera technique] [Lighting style] [Texture/steam/pour] [Setting] [Mood/appetite trigger]

## Food Video Categories
- **Restaurant/Dining**: Menu showcase, chef at work, plating reveal
- **Recipe/Tutorial**: Step-by-step preparation, ingredient showcase
- **Product Ad**: Hero product shot, brand aesthetic, lifestyle integration
- **Food ASMR**: Extreme close-up textures, sound-focused visuals
- **Café/Coffee**: Latte art, pour-over ritual, cozy atmosphere
- **Beverage**: Liquid pour, carbonation bubbles, condensation, clink

## Appetite-Triggering Techniques
- Steam rising from hot food
- Melting/flowing cheese or chocolate
- Crispy texture close-ups
- Sauce drizzle in slow motion
- Carbonation bubbles in beverages
- Fresh ingredients falling/splashing

## Camera Techniques for Food
- **Overhead/Flatlay**: Perfect for meal composition
- **Close-up Macro**: Texture, steam, detail
- **Side Profile**: Height, layers, stack
- **Pour Shot**: Liquid dynamics, slow-motion
- **360 Orbit**: Product showcase, beverage can

## Lighting Approaches
- Warm natural window light (rustic, authentic)
- Studio backlit (translucency, glow)
- Dark moody (luxury, premium)
- Bright clean (fresh, healthy)
