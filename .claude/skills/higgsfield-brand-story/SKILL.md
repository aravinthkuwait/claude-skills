# Seedance 2.0 on Higgsfield — Brand Story Video Generator

## Overview
This skill creates compelling brand storytelling and narrative video prompts for Seedance 2.0 on Higgsfield. Covers origin stories, mission films, company culture, founder narratives, and brand anthems.

## Prompt Structure
[Brand type] [Story arc] [Emotional journey] [Visual style] [Character/protagonist] [Key message] [Tone/voice]

## Brand Story Frameworks
- **Origin Story**: "How it started" journey from problem → founder → solution
- **Mission Statement**: Visual representation of company purpose and values
- **Customer Transformation**: Before/after showing customer impact
- **Founder's Journey**: Personal narrative humanizing the brand
- **Brand Anthem**: Aspirational emotion-driven brand film without hard sell

## Emotional Arcs
- **Struggle → Triumph**: Overcoming challenges, resilience
- **Curiosity → Discovery**: Innovation journey, breakthrough moments
- **Problem → Solution**: Customer pain → brand relief
- **Past → Future**: Heritage meets innovation
- **Individual → Community**: One person's story becomes many

## Visual Storytelling Techniques
- Cinematic documentary style with interview overlay
- Split-screen comparing past and present
- Time-lapse of growth and progress
- Intimate behind-the-scenes moments
- Sweeping landscape establishing shots
- Close-up human details (hands at work, eyes lighting up)

## Tone Options
Inspirational/uplifting, authentic/raw, professional/polished, warm/human, bold/ambitious

## Example Prompts
1. "Brand origin story video, montage of humble garage beginnings to modern office, warm nostalgic color grading, founders shown in action not talking heads, inspirational music build, emotional crescendo"
2. "Company mission film, diverse team working with purpose, intimate close-up moments of impact, sweeping landscape establishing shots, authentic documentary aesthetic, aspirational but grounded tone"
3. "Brand anthem video, visual poem about why the company exists, no product shots, pure emotion, slow-motion meaningful moments, cinematic music, leaves viewer feeling inspired"
