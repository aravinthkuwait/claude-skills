# Seedance 2.0 on Higgsfield — Music Video Generator

## Overview
This skill creates music video and beat-synced visual content prompts for Seedance 2.0 on Higgsfield. Covers performance videos, lyric videos, abstract visualizers, and concept music films.

## Prompt Structure
[Genre/mood] [Artist/performer type] [Visual concept] [Beat sync points] [Setting/environment] [Color treatment] [Camera style]

## Music Video Genres
- **Pop**: Colorful sets, fashion-forward, high-energy choreography
- **Hip-Hop/Rap**: Urban locations, flex aesthetic, crew shots
- **Rock/Metal**: Raw performance energy, moody lighting, stadium or garage
- **EDM/Electronic**: Abstract visuals, particle effects, rave aesthetic
- **R&B/Soul**: Intimate, cinematic, warm tones, emotional storytelling
- **Indie/Alternative**: Artistic concept, unique locations, DIY aesthetic

## Beat Sync Techniques
- Hard cut on beat drops
- Flash effects on snare hits
- Camera shake on bass hits
- Smooth slow-mo between beats
- Color shift on chorus sections

## Visual Treatments
- **Performance**: Artist lip-sync or instrument performance
- **Narrative**: Story-driven visual interpretation of lyrics
- **Abstract**: Non-literal visual art synced to music
- **Lyric Video**: Animated text over visuals
- **Collage**: Mixed media, archival footage, artistic montage

## Location Archetypes
Rooftop at sunset, underground club, empty warehouse, neon-lit streets, desert isolation, luxury penthouse, natural landscape
