# Higgsfield Cinematic Video Skill

Generate high-quality cinematic video prompts for Higgsfield AI / Seedance 2.0.

## 2-Second Hook Framework

Every cinematic video must open with a compelling hook in the first 2 seconds:
1. **Visual hook**: Striking image, unexpected element, or dramatic composition
2. **Motion hook**: Camera movement that immediately signals cinematic quality
3. **Emotional hook**: Mood established through lighting and atmosphere

## Prompt Structure

```
[Hook: 2-second opening action] [Main scene description] [Camera movement] [Lighting] [Mood/atmosphere] [Technical specs]
```

## Camera Encyclopedia

| Movement | Effect | Best For |
|----------|--------|----------|
| Slow dolly in | Intimacy, tension | Character moments |
| Crane up | Epic scale, revelation | Establishing shots |
| Handheld follow | Urgency, realism | Action sequences |
| Static wide | Isolation, grandeur | Landscape, atmosphere |
| Dutch angle | Unease, stylization | Thriller, noir |
| Rack focus | Perspective shift | Dialogue, reveals |

## Production Prompts

### Drama
"Golden hour light streams through dusty warehouse windows, protagonist stands silhouetted against the glow, slow dolly forward, shallow depth of field, film grain, 35mm aesthetic, cinematic color grade"

### Action
"Handheld camera chase through rain-soaked alley, neon reflections on wet pavement, fast cuts implied, motion blur, high contrast, night photography look"

### Landscape Epic
"Crane shot rises above ancient forest at dawn, fog rolls through valleys, golden light breaks through clouds, sweeping orchestral implied, aerial cinematography"

## Best Practices

- Always specify lighting: golden hour, blue hour, overcast, artificial neon, candlelight
- Include camera lens feel: wide angle, telephoto, anamorphic, 35mm, 85mm portrait
- Add texture: film grain, lens flare, chromatic aberration for authenticity
- Specify aspect ratio for cinematic feel: 2.35:1 or 16:9 widescreen
- Mention color grade style: teal-orange, desaturated, high contrast, muted pastels
